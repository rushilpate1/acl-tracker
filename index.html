Patellar graft reality
The donor site sits directly in the force path of deep knee flexion. Anterior knee pain is tissue signaling, not a mental block. Ham graft athletes start pistols at week 12–14. Week 16–18 is your safe window.
First pistol target: week 16–18 (Apr 7–21)
Use the next 3 weeks to build prerequisites. Click each phase to expand.
Now to Week 16 · Mar 23 – Apr 7
Phase 0 — PrerequisitesActive now
▼
No deep flexion. 15 minutes daily. Build motor pattern and ankle mobility so week 16 is just adding depth. TKE and wall sit are in your gym sessions — skip those here on training days.
Single-leg box sit (above parallel)
3 × 8 each · daily
Box just above knee height. 3-sec lower, touch lightly, drive back up. Never past 90°. Motor control and light quad load without touching the donor site range.
Push the floor away going up. Squeeze the glute hard at lockout.
Wall-assisted single-leg lowering
3 × 6 left · daily
Fingertips on wall, balance only. Right leg extended. Bend left knee to 70–80°, hold 2 sec, return. Builds eccentric quad strength at safe depth. Knee tracks over the second toe — no caving.
Imagine a laser on your kneecap pointing forward. Keep it tracking straight.
Ankle dorsiflexion stretch
2 × 40s each · daily
Lunge position, drive knee forward over pinky toe keeping heel down. Tight ankles cause forward lean during pistols and shift load to the knee. Fix this now or it'll be your limiter at week 16.
Heel must stay glued to the floor. If it lifts, back off 1 cm and hold there.
If you feel anterior knee pain during wall lowering even at 70°, stay at pain-free range. Preparation, not provocation.
Week 16–17 · Apr 7–14
Phase 1 — High box pistol
▶
First real pistol reps. Box limits depth to a safe range. Zero anterior knee pain is the standard — not manageable, zero.
High box pistol squat
3 × 5 left only · every other day
Box at knee height. Sit back not down. 3–4 sec lower, lightly touch, drive up through heel. Keeping shin more vertical reduces donor site shear dramatically. Ice 10 min after. 90s rest between sets.
"Reach hips back toward a chair behind you" — not "press knee forward."
Counterbalance hold at bottom
3 × 3 holds · 3 sec each · left
Hold 2–5 lb weight extended in front. Lower to box, hold 3 sec. Counterbalance shifts center of mass forward and actually reduces knee stress. Builds bottom-position confidence.
Holding weight out front reduces donor site load — counterintuitive but true.
Stop if: sharp pain at donor site or increased swelling next morning. General soreness is fine. Sharp anterior pain = one more week of phase 0.
Week 17–19 · Apr 14–28
Phase 2 — Progressive box lowering
▶
Drop box 2–3 inches every 2–3 sessions only if last depth was pain-free. By end of phase, box is at calf height. 3-sec eccentric every rep is mandatory.
Progressive box pistol
3 × 5 left · every other day · drop box 2–3" per 2–3 sessions
Start at knee height, progress to mid-shin, then calf height over 2 weeks. Two pain-free sessions at each depth before dropping. Track the height each session.
Rushing depth is how you get patellar tendinopathy on top of ACL recovery. Pain-free × 2, no exceptions.
Pistol negative (eccentric only)
3 × 4 left · 5-sec lowering · end of session
Lower to full pistol depth over 5 seconds, use both legs to stand back up. Most powerful hypertrophy method with less graft strain than full reps.
Use TRX for balance. The slow lowering is the entire point.
Morning-after swelling is your best indicator. Puffier than normal = that depth was too much. Stay at that height two more sessions.
Week 19–22 · Apr 28 – May 19
Phase 3 — Freestanding pistol
▶
Box comes out. Goal by end of phase: 5 clean unassisted reps on the left leg. Donor site sensitivity should be reducing significantly around weeks 18–20.
TRX-assisted freestanding pistol
3 × 5 left · weeks 19–20
Hold TRX with just enough tension to not fall — offload ~10–15%. No box. Full depth, 1-sec pause at bottom, drive up. Non-working leg extended parallel to floor throughout.
Tight hip flexors cause the free leg to droop — stretch them after every session.
Freestanding pistol squat
Build to 3 × 5 left · weeks 21–22
No assistance. Arms forward as counterbalance. Slow lower, 1-sec pause at bottom, drive up through heel. Goal: 5 clean reps by end of week 22.
If you fall you'll fall forward — that means weight is in the right place (heel).
If anterior knee pain returns when the box is removed, go back to a 4-inch box for one more week. Normal variance.
Week 22–26 · May 19 – Jun 26
Phase 4 — Build to 10 repsAppointment goal
▶
Add 1–2 reps per week. Clean mechanics matter as much as the number. A controlled 8 is more convincing than a sloppy 10.
Pistol rep progression
Wk22: 2×5 · Wk23: 2×6 · Wk24: 2×7 · Wk25: 1×10 · Wk26: test set
Add 1 rep per set per week. Week 26: 10 clean reps, left leg, no assistance. Check: full depth (hip crease below knee), free leg parallel to floor, nothing touched.
Do the test set 2–3 days before the appointment. Show up rested.
